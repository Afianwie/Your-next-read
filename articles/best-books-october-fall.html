Best Books to Read This October Fall

October 04, 2025

October is here, and with the crisp fall air comes the perfect excuse to curl up with a book that matches the season’s eerie, cozy vibe. Whether you’re into witches, vampires, or heartfelt tales, this list has something to warm your soul—or send a chill down your spine. I’ve gathered my top picks for this autumn, including one I’m reading right now, to help you find your next fall obsession. Let’s dive in!

1. Franklin’s A Witch’s Tale

A spellbinding journey into witchcraft that’s perfect for October’s mystical mood. It’s got all the magic and mystery you need for a fall night.

2. Anathema by Keri Lake

Dark and gripping, this tale of suspense fits the shadowy fall atmosphere like a glove. Prepare for a thrilling ride!

3. The Southern Book Club Guide to Slaying Vampires by Grady Hendrix

A hilarious yet spooky take on vampire hunting in the South. It’s a quirky must-read that blends humor with October chills.

4. Rise of the Tucraw by Rowena Graham

This epic fantasy brings a fresh twist to fall reading. Dive into a world of adventure that’s ideal for the season.

5. Here There Are Monsters by Amelinda Bérubé

A haunting story that’ll keep you on edge—perfect for those crisp October evenings when the shadows grow long.

6. Alchemised by Senlinyu

A unique blend of alchemy and intrigue, this book adds a touch of the unusual to your fall reading list.

7. Daisy Darker by Alice Feeney

With its twisty plot and eerie setting, this is a fall thriller that’ll keep you guessing till the last page.

8. The Pumpkin Spice Café by x

Embrace the cozy side of fall with this warm, spiced-up tale. It’s like a pumpkin latte in book form—delightful!

9. Here There Are Monsters by Amelinda Bérubé

Worth a second mention—its chilling vibe is so perfect for October, I couldn’t leave it out twice!

10. The Quiet Tenant

A tense, atmospheric read that pairs beautifully with the quiet, reflective days of fall. You’ll be hooked!

11. The Extraordinary Life of Sam Hell

I’m reading this one now, and it’s shaping up to be a heartfelt journey. Its emotional depth makes it a great fall companion.

12. Demon Copperhead by Barbara Kingsolver

A powerful, gritty story that adds a soulful layer to your October reads. It’s a masterpiece worth savoring.

These books capture the essence of October—spooky, cozy, and deeply moving. Whether you’re sipping cider by the fire or braving the autumn chill, there’s a story here for you. Pick one, dive in, and let me know which fall read becomes your favorite this season!
